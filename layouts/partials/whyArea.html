Choosing IEP Consulting BCBA means

partnering with a team that is invested in your child’s success and well-being, every step of the way.

  • Our multidisciplinary team of BCBAs, Speech Pathologists, Licensed Clinical Social Workers, Certified Psychologists, and Registered Behavior Technicians brings global experience and a deep understanding of diverse needs.

  • We believe that every child is unique. Our approach centers around individualized Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) therapy, designed specifically to address each child's strengths and areas for growth.

  • We work hand-in-hand with families, ensuring they are an integral part of the therapy process. Together, we develop goals and strategies that extend beyond therapy sessions into daily routines.

  • Understanding and respecting cultural differences is at the core of our care. Our team includes multilingual clinicians who are fluent in languages such as Spanish, French, Romanian, and Arabic, allowing us to provide culturally responsive services that meet the unique needs of diverse families.

  • Our programs focus on enhancing communication, social interaction, and everyday life skills. Whether it's building friendships or improving self-care, we provide the tools children need to succeed in their personal and social environments.

  • As a clinician-owned and operated organization, we are committed to delivering compassionate, research-driven therapy that makes a lasting difference. Our mission is to support each child’s growth and development through innovative, effective care.
